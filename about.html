Background

Growing up on the east coast of South Africa, Kate enjoyed the warm winters and suffered through the hot, humid summers. After spending a few years on the other side of the country (where delicious wine is made!), Kate's husband convinced her to leave the vineyards and relocate to a tiny island in the Indian Ocean, Mauritius. Here, they have created a beautiful home with their baby girl and fox terrier, Odie.

Who's walking who?

As much as Kate enjoys her job, sitting is supposed to be the new smoking so she tries to get up and about us much as possible. She enjoys taking Odie on walks, focusing her mind on yoga and getting her heart pumping with some fast-paced exercise routines.

Kate was never any good at the fact-based subjects so she was relieved to find she had a knack for languages. At school, she studied English, French and Zulu.

Arriving at the University of Cape Town, she was unsure of what subjects to study. Kate was nervous that focusing her studies on languages wouldn't lead to a prosperous future. However, during orientation, she found the bravery she lacked when a dashing English lecturer encouraged the first-year students to follow their hearts (be damned what anyone else thought!). Heeding this advice, Kate signed up for English, French and German majors (with a course of Psychology just in case).

Studying languages at UCT was a dream come true. Examining the greats of English literature in the bustling library and exploring language on a daily basis - what more could a young linguaphile want?

But the learning didn't stop with graduation! Language is dynamic. Kate loves to read and keep up with the newest developments. She enjoys uncovering new skills too. Currently, she is expanding her knowledge of copywriting and SEO.

Kate's love of English makes her the perfect person to assist you with all your content needs.

Copywriting, content writing, editing.
She does it all!
